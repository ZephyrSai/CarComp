Qatar 2026 — Which One?

These three flagships serve genuinely different buyers. Jetour G700 is the dedicated desert warrior — body-on-frame, turboprops, 900 mm wading, Tank Turn, Crab Walk, and the longest warranty in segment at the lowest flagship price. BYD Leopard 8 is the family off-roader — 7 seats, DiSus-P active suspension raising to 310 mm, proven Qatar dealer network, and best-in-class fridge and V2L utility. Zeekr 9X Hyper is the luxury performance machine — 1,381 hp, 3.1s, 240 km/h, 900V charging in 6 minutes, and L3-ready ADAS with 5 LiDAR units.

For Qatar's terrain — dune bashing, wadi crossings, deep sand recovery — both the G700 and Leopard 8 are purpose-built. The Zeekr is emphatically not. Between the two BOF rivals, the G700 wins on raw capability and price; the Leopard 8 wins on family space and service assurance.

🔵 Choose G700 if…

  • Serious desert off-road use
  • 6-seat layout is sufficient
  • Best value flagship (QAR 219K)
  • 800V fast charging matters
  • Turboprops for sand recovery
  • Best warranty in segment
  • Huawei ADS 4.0 preferred

🔴 Choose Leopard 8 if…

  • 7 seats / 3rd row required
  • 310 mm raised clearance for dunes
  • Proven BYD Qatar service network
  • Blade LFP battery transparency
  • Built-in fridge + 6 kW V2L
  • Devialet audio + 50" HUD
  • 1,200 km range preferred

🟣 Choose Zeekr 9X if…

  • Max performance: 1,381 hp / 3.1s
  • L3-ready ADAS with 5 LiDAR
  • 900V · fastest charging (~6 min)
  • 240 km/h highway capability
  • Primarily on-road luxury use
  • Zero-gravity executive seating
  • Budget allows est. QAR 275K
