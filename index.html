Qatar 2026 Recommendation

At flagship level, the Jetour G700 is the stronger choice for Qatar. It delivers 166 more horsepower, 375 more Nm of torque, a generational advantage in charging speed (800V vs 400V), military turboprops, Crab Walk, a cinema-grade cockpit, and the longest warranty in its class — all at a lower price than the Leopard 8 Flagship.

The BYD Leopard 8 makes a compelling case on three specific grounds: its DiSus-P active suspension raising ground clearance to 310 mm is genuinely exceptional for dune driving; its 7-seat 3-row layout is the only true family-of-seven option; and BYD's established Qatar dealer network gives peace of mind that Jetour, as a newer entrant, cannot yet match.

For a buyer where performance, technology, and value define the decision — the G700 wins. For a buyer where third-row seating and long-term service assurance are the priority — the Leopard 8 is the right call.

🔵 Choose G700 Flagship if…

  • Maximum performance is the priority
  • 6-seat layout is sufficient
  • Save QAR 11–25K vs Leopard 8
  • 800V fast charging matters
  • Turboprops for deep desert recovery
  • Best warranty package in segment
  • Cutting-edge Huawei ADS 4.0

🔴 Choose Leopard 8 if…

  • 7 seats / 3rd row is required
  • 310 mm raised clearance for dunes
  • Proven BYD service in Qatar
  • Blade LFP battery transparency
  • 1,200 km range is preferred
  • Built-in fridge + V2L for camping
  • Devialet audio is a priority
